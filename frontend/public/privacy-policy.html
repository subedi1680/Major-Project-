Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 13, 2025

Your Privacy Matters! This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information on JobBridge. We believe in transparency and keeping things simple.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information:
  • Name and email address
  • Password (encrypted and secure)
  • User type (job seeker or employer)
Profile Information (Optional):
  • Profile picture
  • Phone number and location
  • Work experience and skills
  • Company information (for employers)
Usage Information:
  • Jobs you view and apply to
  • Login times and activity
  • Device and browser information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our service: Match job seekers with employers
  • Improve your experience: Show relevant jobs and candidates
  • Keep you secure: Protect against fraud and abuse
  • Communicate with you: Send important updates and notifications
  • Provide support: Help when you have questions or issues

3. Information Sharing

We DO NOT sell your personal information. We only share information when:

  • You apply for a job: Your application goes to that employer
  • You post a job: Job seekers can see your job posting
  • Legal requirements: When required by law (very rare)
  • Service providers: Trusted partners who help run our platform (with strict privacy agreements)

4. Your Privacy Controls

You have full control over your information:

  • Profile visibility: Choose what information to share
  • Email preferences: Control what notifications you receive
  • Data access: View all information we have about you
  • Data deletion: Delete your account and data anytime
  • Data correction: Update or correct your information

5. Data Security

We protect your information with:

  • Encryption: Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Secure servers: Industry-standard security measures
  • Access controls: Only authorized staff can access data
  • Regular audits: We regularly review our security practices
  • Session management: Automatic logout when you close your browser

6. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal tracking:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to work (login, security)
  • No advertising cookies: We don't track you for ads
  • No third-party tracking: We don't share data with ad networks
  • Analytics: Basic usage statistics to improve our service (anonymized)

7. Data Retention

How long we keep your information:

  • Active accounts: As long as your account is active
  • Deleted accounts: 30 days (for recovery), then permanently deleted
  • Job applications: Until you or the employer deletes them
  • Legal requirements: Some data may be kept longer if required by law

8. Children's Privacy

JobBridge is for adults (18+) seeking employment. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

9. International Users

If you're outside the United States:

  • Your data may be processed in the US where our servers are located
  • We comply with applicable international privacy laws
  • You have the same privacy rights regardless of location

10. Changes to This Policy

When we update this policy:

  • We'll notify you via email before changes take effect
  • We'll highlight what changed
  • You can review the new policy before it applies
  • Continued use means you accept the updated policy

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: See what information we have about you
  • Correct: Fix any incorrect information
  • Delete: Remove your account and data
  • Export: Get a copy of your data
  • Object: Opt out of certain data processing

12. Contact Us About Privacy

Questions about your privacy or this policy?

  • Email: jobbridge123@gmail.com
  • Response time: We'll get back to you within 48 hours
  • Account settings: Many privacy controls are in your account settings
Our Promise: We're committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your information. Your trust is important to us!
